📊 Global Superstore Sales Power BI Dashboard
A multi-page Power BI dashboard delivering a full 360° analysis of a global retail superstore — covering sales performance, profitability, and order quantities across markets, regions, categories, and customer segments.
📌 Pages
1. Sales Analysis
High-level overview with $12.6M in total sales, $1.5M profit, 178.3K units, and 17.4K unique customers. Breakdowns by market (area chart), region, category, customer segment (donut), and top 8 customers.
2. Profit Analysis
Profitability deep-dive with $244K total profit and $227 average profit. Views by year, global market, region, product category (Technology leads at $107K), customer segment, and top 8 most profitable customers.
3. Quantity Analysis
Volume-focused page tracking 178K units sold with 1,079 return orders. Quantity trends by year, market, region, category (Office Supplies dominates at 108K), segment, and top 8 customers by order volume.
✨ Features

Consistent layout across all 3 pages for easy comparison
Sub Category and Year slicers for dynamic filtering
Area/funnel-style market charts across 5 global markets (Asia Pacific, Europe, USCA, LATAM, Africa)
Donut charts for Consumer / Corporate / Home Office segment split
Top 8 rankings for both regions and customers
Three distinct color themes per page: blue, teal/green, and pink/purple

🛠️ Tools & Tech

Power BI Desktop
DAX (KPI measures, ranking, time intelligence)
Star schema data model (orders, customers, products, geography)

📁 Dataset
Based on the classic Global Superstore dataset — a widely-used retail dataset covering orders from 2012–2015 across categories: Technology, Furniture, and Office Supplies
